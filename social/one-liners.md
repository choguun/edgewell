### Alt-text (1 paragraph)

A laptop, a Raspberry Pi 4, and a phone are huddled together on a
desk, lit by a single warm key light, with a thought-bubble above
the phone showing a small orchestrator graph — six specialist
icons (Health, Finance, Sleep, Nutrition, Hydration, Activity)
fanning out from a single router node, with a tiny green shield
icon in the corner signalling local-first encryption. The
composition reads as "the same software, three form factors, one
private brain" and is the single visual that anchors every social
